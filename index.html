About Us

Little Italy was born with the intent to recreate the Italian family environment offering authentic Italian food with traditional recipes and genuine ingredients. “The simplicity is the most difficult thing to secure”. Italian cuisine is generally made of just few good ingredients per dish rather than elaborated preparations. Little Italy is the place where to be friends and slow down with a good bottle of wine.

Our Chef

CHEF CARLOS was born in Brazil from an Italian family, originally from Trento. He had left Brazil to pursue his career as a Chef specialised in Italian cuisine since young. He loves travelling and exploring new food and ingredients during his trip. He had travelled to many countries and ever worked in UK, America and Russia. He decided to settle down in Singapore 2 years ago. He was formerly the chef from In Piazza on Steven Road. Say hi to him if you spot him in the restaurant, do not feel shy to ask him for recommendation.
